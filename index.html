Privacy Policy for Majalgaon Directory Last Updated: June 2026 Prashilkumar P. Khurpe ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Majalgaon Directory mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data. 1. Information We Collect To provide and improve our local directory services, we may collect the following information when you register an account or interact with the App: - Personal Profile Information: Full Name, Phone Number, Date of Birth (optional), Email Address (optional), and Password. - Contributions and Reviews: User-generated content, ratings, reviews, and comments regarding local businesses and services. - Connection Information: Basic log data related to synchronization requests with our secure cloud servers. 2. How We Use Your Information We use the collected data for various purposes: - To provide, maintain, and secure the App's directory and sync features. - To verify your identity and manage your registered user account. - To attribute review comments and ratings with your display name to maintain a community-vetted, reliable listing ecosystem. - To allow secure offline storage via local Room databases and seamless cloud-sync to MongoDB Atlas. 3. Sharing and Disclosure We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal identification info with any third-party marketing companies, advertisers, or secondary organizations. Your data is strictly used for the core directory services of Majalgaon Directory. 4. Data Security and Offline Mode The App is built with an offline-first design pattern. Most information is saved on your device's secure sandbox using SQLite Room databases. When you perform cloud synchronization, your account details and contributed reviews are transmitted securely over SSL/TLS encrypted pipelines to our secure server clusters. 5. Permissions and Access Our App does NOT declare or access any dangerous system permissions. It only requests the standard INTERNET permission to perform essential data synchronization when a network connection is available. No location tracking, camera access, storage permissions, or contact list reads are performed. 6. Children's Privacy Our App does not address or target anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will immediately delete it from our servers. 7.
